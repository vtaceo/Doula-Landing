Get to Know Me

Hi, I'm Marlena

Understanding my background before reaching out is probably important to you… And it’s important to me, too.

Here’s a brief summary of who I am:

My name is Marlena Matheson and I support families in Seattle and the surrounding areas. For 15 years, I have been serving mothers and families during the pregnancy, labor, and postnatal process. I understand the importance of inclusivity, cultural difference, and personal choice—and love working with all kinds of growing families.

When I’m not supporting mothers and families, I enjoy hiking, painting, and spending quality time with my husband and kids.
