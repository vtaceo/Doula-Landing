Services

I offer emotional and physical support during pregnancy, birth, and postpartum. My goal is to ensure mothers and families in Seattle experience a fulfilling prenatal and birth journey — and feel secure and loved along the way.

Birth Planning

I'm committed to helping you navigate the exceptionally personal and magical journey into parenthood! As you search for a doula, you need someone with experience, expertise, and compassion. No matter your background, I am here to support you.

Prenatal Support

From week to week, or month to month, you might have questions along the way of your pregnancy journey. Let me support you.

Pregnancy Doula Services

During pregnancy, especially if it’s your first child. You may feel overwhelmed by the amount of resources out there and finding a source for accurate answers. Come no further. We are here to support you. This includes the proper education during the pregnancy process, all the way through postpartum life.

In-Person Birth Support

We provide physical support during the labor and birthing process. My sole responsibility is to care for you—and to let you direct your birth story. You are the author of your story, and I will help you make it come to life.

PHYSICAL SUPPORT

  • Physical touch, like massage, pressure, assisting you with toileting and being on call during the intense times.
  • Guiding you and your partner through labor by suggesting techniques for pain management and also by explaining medical procedures in layman’s terms.
  • Creating a soothing environment.

EMOTIONAL SUPPORT

  • Caring for the mother and partner’s emotional wellbeing through encouragement, mirroring, educated guidance, positive affirmation, and advocacy. I will help you feel empowered to make the right decisions about your own body and your baby.

Postpartum Doula

We tailor our methods for your unique situation during the postpartum period. The goal is to nurture you and your baby as you step into the fourth trimester. We can help squash obstacles you’ll face at home with your new baby, from sibling introductions and light housework to postnatal care.

Virtual Visits

We can meet with you in a manner you feel comfortable with, including virtually (email, video call, text, or phone call).
